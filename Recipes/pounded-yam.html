POUNDED YAM

Description

Pounded yam and vegeatble soup is a very good combo. Pounded Yam is an awesome African dish, made from a tuber crop called Yam which is rich in carbohydrate.

Vegetables Soup is a nutritious soup which is made from vegetables and its rich in vitamins, protein, fats and oil.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Yam
  • Green Vegetables
  • Meat
  • Dry fish
  • Red oil
  • Tomato and pepper
  • Spices

Steps to Prepare Pounded Yam

  1. Put water in a cooking put and place on fire to get warm
  2. Peal off the back of the yam and wash properly
  3. Cut the yam tuber into small pieces and put inside the water while still on fire to get boiled and cooked
  4. Once cooked, take it off the fire and put a morter and pound using a pistle till it get soft
  5. Done and ready for consumption.
