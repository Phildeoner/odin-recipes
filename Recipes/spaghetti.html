SPAGHETTI

Description

Spaghetti is a recipe rich in differnt nutrients.

This recipe can be prepared in many ways using different ingredients and therfore contain different nutrients such as carbohydrate, vitamins, protein, fats and oil.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Spaghetti
  • Fresh or Dry fish
  • Meat
  • Small vegetable
  • Groundnut or any cooking oil
  • Tomato and pepper
  • Spices

Steps to Prepare Spaghetti

  1. Put water in a cooking put and place on fire to get warm
  2. Pour in the Spaghetti in the water while still of fire so it boils and left half cooked
  3. Pour in a basket to take away the water
  4. Put a cooking oil in the pot and place on fire
  5. Mix the ingredients and add water that will be enough to finish the cooking process
  6. Allow it to boil for the ingredients to mix properly
  7. Pour in the boiled spaghetti and stir properly then allow it get cooked and once the water is drained you put down from the fire.
  8. Done and ready for Consumption
