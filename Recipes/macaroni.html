MACARONI

Description

Macaroni is a recipe which contain majorly carbohydrate in raw state

This recipe can contain more nutrients based on how its been prepared and nourished such as vitamins, protein, fats and oil.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Macaroni
  • Vegetables
  • Egg
  • Dry or fresh fish
  • Red oil or Groundnut oil
  • Tomato and pepper
  • Spices

Steps to Prepare Macaroni

  1. Put water in a cooking put and place on fire to get warm
  2. Pour in the macaroni in the water while still of fire so it boils and left half cooked
  3. Pour in a basket to take away the water
  4. Put a cooking oil in the pot and place on fire
  5. Mix the ingredients and add water that will be enough to finish the cooking process
  6. Allow it to boil for the ingredients to mix properly
  7. Pour in the boiled macaroni and stir properly then allow it get cooked and once the water is drained you put down from the fire.
  8. Done and ready for Consumption
